WHY WE'RE HERE

Our mission? To make sure good food gets eaten, not wasted. Every day, delicious, fresh food goes to waste at cafés, restaurants, hotels, shops and manufacturers - just because it hasn’t sold in time. Still Good lets customers buy food - at a great price - directly from businesses.

More than 1/3 of food is wasted

and we want to change that
